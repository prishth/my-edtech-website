🎯 CMAT 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
Ace General Awareness for Easy Marks: GA carries 80 marks but requires zero exam skills — just knowledge. Read newspapers, follow current affairs apps, and cover static GK to score 60+ in GA easily.
2
Innovation Section is a Differentiator: Most students ignore the Innovation & Entrepreneurship section. Spending 20–30 minutes daily on startup news and business case studies can get you 60+ marks here.
3
CMAT Quant is CAT-Level Simpler: The Quant questions in CMAT are easier than CAT. Focus on arithmetic topics like percentages, ratios, TSD, and profit & loss for maximum returns.
4
Attempt All 100 Questions: With a 3-hour duration for 100 questions, you have ample time. Never leave questions unanswered — even an educated guess is worth trying with +4 reward.
5
Prepare for GE-PI-WAT after CMAT: Top CMAT colleges like JBIMS and GIM Goa have rigorous GD/PI rounds. Start essay writing and current affairs preparation alongside CMAT prep.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — CMAT 2026
Which colleges accept CMAT scores?
JBIMS Mumbai, SIMSREE Mumbai, KJ Somaiya, GIM Goa, Welingkar Mumbai, PUMBA Pune, IMT Nagpur, BIMTECH, and 1000+ AICTE-approved management institutes accept CMAT scores.
Is CMAT easier than CAT?
Yes. CMAT is considerably easier than CAT. The questions are more straightforward and the General Awareness and Innovation sections can be high-scoring areas.
What is a good CMAT score for JBIMS?
JBIMS Mumbai is the most prestigious CMAT-accepting college. You typically need 99.5+ percentile (350+ marks out of 400) to be considered for JBIMS.
What is the Innovation & Entrepreneurship section?
This is a unique CMAT section not found in any other MBA exam. It tests knowledge of the startup ecosystem, business innovation concepts, and entrepreneurship case studies.
