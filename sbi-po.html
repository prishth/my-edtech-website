🎯 SBI PO 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
High-Level Puzzles are the Key: SBI PO Mains has the most complex puzzles and seating arrangements in any banking exam. Practice 5–6 high-difficulty puzzle sets daily in the last 2 months.
2
Current Affairs Must Be 6 Months Deep: SBI PO Mains asks questions from at least the last 6 months of current affairs. Follow daily CA capsules and cover RBI policies, government schemes, and economic data.
3
Descriptive Writing Practice: SBI PO Mains includes a Letter and Essay writing component (50 marks). Practice at least 2 descriptive essays per week from 60 days before the exam.
4
Data Interpretation Speed: SBI PO DI sets are calculation-intensive. Practice DI sets of all types (Caselet, Missing DI, Double Pie) daily and master percentage-to-fraction shortcuts.
5
Give Full-Length Mocks: Start full-length mocks from Month 2. SBI PO tests stamina across a long exam. Regular mock analysis is more important than just attempting mocks.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — SBI PO 2026
What is the SBI PO 2026 vacancy count?
SBI has announced 1,500 vacancies for Probationary Officer posts under Advertisement No. CRPD/PO/2026-27/09.
Is there negative marking in SBI PO?
Yes. In Prelims, there is 1/4th negative marking (0.25 marks) for each wrong answer. In Mains, the negative marking is as per the objective sections.
What is the SBI PO selection process?
SBI PO has 3 phases: Phase 1 (Prelims — 100 marks), Phase 2 (Mains — 250 marks including descriptive), and Phase 3 (Psychometric Test, Group Exercise, and Personal Interview).
What is the SBI PO in-hand salary?
SBI PO starting in-hand salary is approximately ₹80,000–₹85,000 per month in metro cities, including DA, HRA, and other allowances.
How many times can I appear for SBI PO?
SBI does not specify a maximum number of attempts for PO recruitment. You can appear as long as you meet the age and eligibility criteria.
