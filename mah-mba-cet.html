MAH MBA CET 2027 — Maharashtra MBA/MMS CET

Conducted by the State CET Cell, Maharashtra. 200 questions, 150 minutes, no negative marking, and the gateway to JBIMS, SIMSREE, PUMBA and Welingkar.

The Maharashtra MBA/MMS Common Entrance Test is a state-level examination conducted by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, for admission to postgraduate management programmes across the state.

It is the gateway to MBA and MMS programmes at government, government-aided, university-managed and unaided institutes approved by AICTE and recognised universities in Maharashtra — including JBIMS, SIMSREE, PUMBA and Welingkar.

Candidates from every state of India are eligible to appear. However, admission procedure, reservation policy and seat allocation vary depending on domicile status and institutional regulation.

✅MAH MBA CET 2027 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must satisfy the requirements prescribed by the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, before applying.

  • Educational qualificationBachelor's degree of minimum three years' duration from a recognised university.
  • Minimum marks50 percent aggregate, relaxed to 45 percent for Maharashtra reserved category and PwD candidates.
  • Final-year studentsCandidates in the final year of graduation are also eligible to apply.
  • StreamGraduates from any discipline can appear for the examination.
  • DomicileCandidates from all states of India are eligible, though seat allocation depends on domicile status.
  • ReservationBenefits apply as per Maharashtra Government norms, and reserved category candidates must produce valid supporting documents.
  • VerificationAdmission is subject to eligibility verification and participation in the Centralised Admission Process.

📚MAH MBA CET 2027 Syllabus

The syllabus assesses aptitude across four areas: Logical Reasoning, Abstract Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude, and Verbal Ability with Reading Comprehension. The focus is on analytical thinking, numerical ability, language proficiency and problem-solving speed.

Key points

  • All questions are multiple choice with five options, not four.
  • There is no negative marking, so every question should be attempted.
  • Test duration is 150 minutes with no sectional time limits.
  • The medium of the examination is English, and the mode is online.

What the weightage actually tells you

Logical and Abstract Reasoning together carry 100 of the 200 marks — exactly half the paper.

75 + 25 = 100 marks from reasoning alone

That is the single most important strategic fact about this exam. A candidate who is strong at quant but average at reasoning is playing for the smaller half. Reasoning speed, not quant depth, is what separates a 99 percentile from a 97 percentile in MAH CET.

📝MAH MBA CET 2027 Registration Process

The application form is available only in online mode through the official website of the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra. Read the official information brochure and user manual before starting, since a large share of rejected forms come from avoidable data errors.

  1. Visit the official website — go to cetcell.mahacet.org. Only online applications are accepted.
  2. Register yourself — use a valid email ID and active mobile number.
  3. Fill the application form — enter personal, academic and communication details.
  4. Verify Aadhaar and APAAR ID — complete authentication via DigiLocker where applicable.
  5. Upload required documents — photograph, signature and relevant certificates in the prescribed format.
  6. Pay the application fee — through the available online payment modes.
  7. Submit and download — save the confirmation page for future reference.

Keep your Application Number safe. It is required for every future correspondence relating to MAH MBA CET 2027, including CAP registration. The State CET Cell may verify your details at any stage, and incorrect or mismatched information can lead to rejection.

❓Frequently Asked Questions — MAH MBA CET 2027

Is there negative marking in MAH MBA CET 2027?

No. MAH MBA CET has no negative marking. Every question should be attempted, since a wrong answer costs nothing and a blank guarantees zero.

How many questions does MAH MBA CET have, and what is the duration?

200 multiple choice questions carrying 1 mark each, to be attempted in 150 minutes. Each question has five options rather than the usual four.

Which section carries the most weight in MAH MBA CET?

Reasoning. Logical Reasoning carries 75 marks and Abstract Reasoning another 25, so reasoning accounts for 100 of the 200 marks — exactly half the paper.

Can candidates from outside Maharashtra appear for MAH MBA CET?

Yes. Candidates from all states of India are eligible to appear. However, seat allocation, reservation policy and the admission procedure depend on domicile status under Maharashtra Government norms.

Are final-year graduation students eligible for MAH MBA CET 2027?

Yes. Candidates in the final year of a bachelor's degree can apply, subject to eligibility verification at the time of admission.

What score is needed for JBIMS Mumbai through MAH MBA CET?

JBIMS typically requires a score of around 155 or above, corresponding to roughly the 99.98 percentile and higher. Actual cutoffs vary each year with paper difficulty and CAP round movement.

What is the CAP process in MAH MBA CET admission?

CAP stands for Centralised Admission Process. After the result, candidates register for CAP, complete document verification, and are allotted seats through merit lists across multiple rounds. Participation in CAP is mandatory for admission to most institutes.

Is MAH MBA CET easier than CAT?

The individual questions are generally less difficult than CAT, but the exam demands far greater speed — 200 questions in 150 minutes works out to under 45 seconds per question. It is a speed test more than a difficulty test.
