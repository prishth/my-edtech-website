🎯 IPMAT Indore 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
Verbal Ability is Your Scoring Key: VA carries 45 questions and 180 marks — the biggest section. IIM Indore VA includes complex RC passages and diverse question types. Practise at least 2 RC passages daily.
2
Short Answer QA Needs Calculation Accuracy: SA questions carry +3 per correct answer with no negative marking. Solve every SA question — they are your guaranteed scoring opportunity.
3
Geometry is Critical in IPMAT QA: IIM Indore QA consistently tests Geometry (triangles, circles, coordinate geometry) heavily. Revise all geometric theorems and properties thoroughly.
4
Practise at IIM Difficulty Level: IPMAT Indore is significantly harder than most BBA entrance exams. Practice CAT-level Quant questions to build the problem-solving speed needed.
5
Start Prep in Class 11: The earlier you start, the better. Students who begin IPMAT preparation in Class 11 have a significant advantage in both Quant concepts and Verbal ability.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — IPMAT Indore 2026
What degree do you get from IIM Indore IPM?
After 5 years, students earn a BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) for the first 3 years and an MBA (Master of Business Administration) from IIM Indore for the last 2 years.
Is there a Personal Interview after IPMAT Indore?
Yes. Clearing IPMAT is the first stage. Shortlisted candidates are called for a Personal Interview (PI), and final selection is based on IPMAT score + PI performance.
Can Arts students apply for IPMAT Indore?
Yes. Students from any stream — Science, Commerce, or Arts/Humanities — can apply for IPMAT Indore.
How many seats are there in IIM Indore IPM?
IIM Indore typically offers around 120 seats in the IPM programme (exact number may vary year to year).
