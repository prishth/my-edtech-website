🎯 IPMAT Rohtak 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
Logical Reasoning is Rohtak's Key Differentiator: IPMAT Rohtak has a full LR section — unlike IIM Indore. Complex puzzles and seating arrangements need daily dedicated practice.
2
Equal Weightage Across 3 Sections: All 3 sections carry equal marks (160 each). Don't neglect any section. A balanced approach is essential unlike Indore where VA dominates.
3
Prepare for Both IPMAT Exams: IPMAT Rohtak and Indore have overlapping syllabi. Preparing for both simultaneously doubles your chances at two prestigious IIM IPM programmes.
4
GD Preparation is Essential: Unlike IPMAT Indore (PI only), Rohtak has a GD round. Practice group discussions on current affairs topics, business cases, and abstract topics.
5
Class 12 Percentage Matters: A strong Class 12 result (90%+) improves your composite score. Don't let board exam preparation slide during IPMAT prep.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — IPMAT Rohtak 2026
How is IPMAT Rohtak different from IPMAT Indore?
IPMAT Rohtak has 3 sections (QA, LR, VA) with 40 questions each, while IPMAT Indore has separate MCQ and Short Answer sections. Rohtak includes a Logical Reasoning section which Indore does not.
Is there a GD/PI after IPMAT Rohtak?
Yes. After IPMAT Rohtak shortlisting, selected candidates appear for Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI). Final selection is based on IPMAT score + GD + PI.
What is unique about IIM Rohtak IPM?
IIM Rohtak's IPM provides the IIM brand on your CV after Class 12. The programme offers direct MBA from IIM Rohtak without appearing for CAT after 3 years of BBA.
How many seats does IIM Rohtak IPM offer?
IIM Rohtak typically offers around 140 seats in its IPM programme.
