🎯 RRB NTPC 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
General Awareness Carries Maximum Marks: GA has the highest weightage (40 marks in CBT 1). Cover both Current Affairs (last 12 months) and static GK including history, geography, and Indian polity.
2
Mathematics Must Be Calculation-Fast: RRB NTPC Maths questions are straightforward but the volume is high. Master mental calculation, percentage fractions, and time-saving shortcuts.
3
CBT 1 is Only for Shortlisting: CBT 1 marks are not counted in the final merit. Only CBT 2 marks determine your final rank. Treat CBT 1 as a qualifying round.
4
Focus on Accuracy, Not Speed: With -1/3 negative marking, one wrong answer costs you 1.33 marks effectively (1 earned + 0.33 lost). Attempt only questions you are confident about.
5
Stay Updated on Railway Affairs: RRB NTPC GA section often includes questions about Indian Railways specifically — new routes, station developments, and rail budget. Track these actively.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — RRB NTPC 2026
What is NTPC full form?
NTPC stands for Non-Technical Popular Categories. These are clerical and administrative posts in Indian Railways that do not require technical qualifications.
How many stages are there in RRB NTPC selection?
RRB NTPC selection has multiple stages: CBT 1 (shortlisting), CBT 2 (merit), followed by Typing Skill Test or Computer Aptitude Test (for relevant posts), and Document Verification & Medical Examination.
Is there negative marking in RRB NTPC?
Yes. There is 1/3 negative marking for each wrong answer in both CBT 1 and CBT 2.
What posts come under RRB NTPC Graduate level?
Graduate level posts include Junior Account Assistant cum Typist, Senior Clerk cum Typist, Junior Time Keeper, Trains Clerk, Station Master, Goods Guard, Senior Commercial cum Ticket Clerk, and others.
Can a 12th pass student apply for RRB NTPC?
Yes. RRB NTPC has separate UG (Under Graduate) posts for 12th pass candidates including Junior Clerk cum Typist, Accounts Clerk cum Typist, Junior Time Keeper, Trains Clerk, and Commercial cum Ticket Clerk.
