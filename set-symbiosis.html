🎯 SET 2026 Preparation Strategy
1
60 Minutes for 60 Questions — Pure Speed Exam: With 1 minute per question and no negative marking, SET rewards speed. Practice solving 15 questions per section in exactly 15 minutes.
2
Attempt Every Question: No negative marking means every blank question is a wasted opportunity. Even guess on questions you're unsure about — your probability of being right is 25% per MCQ.
3
GK and Current Affairs are Low Hanging Fruit: 15 questions from GK take just 5 minutes to answer but require weeks of preparation. Follow daily news for 2 months before the exam.
4
Both Dates Strategy: Appear for Test 1 on May 2 as your primary attempt. Use the experience to identify areas for improvement and score better on Test 2 on May 10.
5
Prepare PI-WAT Alongside SET: Top SET percentile means nothing without a strong PI-WAT. Practice extempore speaking, essay writing, and industry knowledge for Symbiosis institutes from Day 1.
❓ Frequently Asked Questions — SET 2026
Which is the best college through SET?
SIBM Pune (Symbiosis Institute of Business Management) is the most prestigious BBA institute accepting SET scores. SCMC and SSMC are also highly regarded for Mass Communication.
Is SET only for BBA?
No. SET is accepted for various programmes including BBA, Mass Communication, Computer Applications, Economics, Applied Statistics, AI, Liberal Arts, and more across 13 Symbiosis institutes.
What happens after SET results?
After SET scores are released, shortlisted candidates are called for a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI) at their chosen Symbiosis institutes.
Can I appear for SET twice?
Yes. SET is conducted on two dates (May 2 and May 10 in 2026). You can appear for both, and the higher score is considered.
